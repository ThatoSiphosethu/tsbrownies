It's more than a Brownie

TS Brownies is a brand founded in early 2020, with an aim to create job opportunities during the Covid-19 epidemic. Over the course of the months, TS' Brownies has won the hearts of the people with our individual chocolate fudge brownies that melt in your mouth filled with different toppings. Our brownies are packaged in a box of either 6, 9 and 18. The box is then bowed and comes with a quote of the day to brighten your day.
